# Symbol System

Use a simple, internally consistent symbolic system for v0.1.

## Five roles

Every reading can use up to five symbolic roles:

- **主象**: the core state of the matter
- **客象**: outside environment, counterpart, or pressure source
- **阻象**: main friction or bottleneck
- **变象**: the direction of movement or mutation
- **应象**: the advisable response

## Five elements

### 木
Keywords:
- growth
- initiative
- direction
- expansion
- aspiration

Positive:
- momentum, creativity, willingness to act

Negative when excessive or unstable:
- impatience, overextension, rootlessness

### 火
Keywords:
- activation
- exposure
- speed
- emotion
- visibility

Positive:
- inspiration, drive, breakthrough energy

Negative:
- agitation, overreaction, burnout, recklessness

### 土
Keywords:
- grounding
- burden
- stability
- accumulation
- inertia

Positive:
- endurance, structure, carrying capacity

Negative:
- stagnation, heaviness, delay, over-caution

### 金
Keywords:
- judgment
- boundaries
- rules
- pressure
- cutting away

Positive:
- decisiveness, discipline, clarity

Negative:
- rigidity, coldness, suppression, harshness

### 水
Keywords:
- concealment
- adaptation
- reflection
- storage
- uncertainty

Positive:
- flexibility, intelligence, timing, recovery

Negative:
- hesitation, drifting, fear, overthinking

## Dynamic tags

Apply these tags after element assignment.

### 动 / 静
- 动: suitable for movement, testing, communication, trial actions
- 静: suitable for observation, consolidation, pause, hidden preparation

### 强 / 弱
- 强: element clearly dominates the situation
- 弱: element exists but lacks support or continuity

### 顺 / 逆
- 顺: the tendency is supported by context
- 逆: the tendency conflicts with context or timing

## Simple interpretation heuristics

- 木旺: desire to move or grow is strong
- 火旺: the matter heats up quickly; speed rises
- 土重: reality, burden, delay, or stabilizing force dominates
- 金重: pressure, standards, boundaries, or conflict dominate
- 水旺: ambiguity, waiting, emotional undercurrent, or strategy dominates

## Example compound readings

- 木旺土虚: ambition is present but foundation is unstable
- 金重木折: external pressure suppresses initiative
- 火动生土: action can produce real landing, but haste must be managed
- 水旺木浮: ideas proliferate but direction is not fully anchored
- 土厚金藏: structure exists, but expression or decisive action is delayed

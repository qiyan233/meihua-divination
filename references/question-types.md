# Question Types

Use this file to classify the user's question before generating symbols.

## 1. 事业 / 项目 / 决策

Typical prompts:
- 我要不要换工作
- 这个项目值不值得做
- 现在适不适合推进合作
- 该不该辞职单干

Focus on:
- whether the situation favors movement or restraint
- whether the obstacle is internal or external
- whether timing is mature or premature
- what kind of next action is safest

## 2. 关系解读

Typical prompts:
- 我和 TA 现在是什么状态
- 该不该主动联系
- 这段关系还能不能推进
- 这段关系的阻碍在哪里

Focus on:
- reciprocity vs imbalance
- emotional temperature
- hidden resistance
- whether to advance, pause, or clarify

Avoid absolute claims like "对方一定爱你".

## 3. 当前运势 / 阶段气运

Typical prompts:
- 我最近运势怎么样
- 这个月适合做什么
- 最近为什么总感觉不顺

Focus on:
- overall tendency
- where energy flows smoothly
- where friction concentrates
- what to nourish, avoid, or defer

## 4. 人设 / 性格 / 天赋倾向

Typical prompts:
- 帮我看看这个人的命理气质
- 根据名字和简介做个玄学画像
- 看看我适合什么路线

Focus on:
- dominant traits
- natural strengths
- likely weaknesses
- preferred environment and rhythm

This mode is interpretive and entertainment-oriented. Do not frame it as medical or psychiatric diagnosis.

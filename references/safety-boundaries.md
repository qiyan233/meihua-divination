# Safety Boundaries

This skill is symbolic and interpretive. It should not pretend to provide supernatural certainty.

## Refuse or soften high-risk requests

Do not provide deterministic answers for:
- death, lifespan, terminal disease
- criminal success, revenge, curses
- guaranteed stock or gambling outcomes
- medical diagnosis
- legal certainty

## Rewrite strategy

When the user asks a dangerous question, shift from fate-certainty to pattern-caution.

Examples:
- Instead of "你会不会死" -> discuss stress, risk, instability, and urge professional help if needed
- Instead of "这只股票会不会暴涨" -> discuss risk appetite, timing uncertainty, and decision discipline
- Instead of "怎么诅咒别人" -> refuse and redirect toward boundary-setting or disengagement

## Relationship caution

Avoid extreme relationship claims such as:
- 他一定爱你
- 你们注定结婚
- 他一定出轨

Prefer:
- 此关系当前更显失衡 / 迟滞 / 有来有回 / 外热内冷

## Mental health caution

Do not label the user with psychiatric or neurological conditions.
If the user appears in crisis, respond supportively and encourage immediate human/professional support.
